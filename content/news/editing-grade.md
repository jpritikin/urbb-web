---
title: "On That A-Minus"
date: 2026-06-22
---

BookLife gave *Religion Unburdened by Belief* an **A-** in editing. I appreciate the **A**, but the **-** stings. As a first-time author, I thought I did everything you're supposed to do: 19 beta readers and professional editor [Nicholas Carter](https://www.fiverr.com/thisisstrange). That's enough, right? Then I recorded the audiobook.

Reading your own prose aloud reveals what silent editing misses—awkward phrasings and redundant clauses. I caught dozens of glitches that had survived every earlier pass. The real revelation came afterward, listening back to the recording with a print copy in front of me—more fixes, more edits. Damn, I was still finding things right up to the offset printer's deadline.

I wish I'd done both *before* submitting to BookLife.

Silver lining: the version available today incorporates all those fixes—more polished than what BookLife and Kirkus reviewed. ✨
