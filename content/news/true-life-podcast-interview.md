Joshua Pritikin joined George Monty on the *True Life Podcast* to discuss *Religion Unburdened by Belief*, exploring the idea that belief itself, not its absence, may be religion's greatest burden. The conversation covers the distinction between content beliefs and process beliefs, why uncertainty can deepen rather than threaten spiritual experience, and how beliefs evolve into knowledge.
